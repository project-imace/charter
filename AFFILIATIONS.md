# Affiliation

Project IMACE (Integrated Modular Architecture for Cognitive Emulation) maintains structured affiliations within the broader [THE ALTERN Research](https://research.thealtern.xyz) ecosystem. [Github](github.com/the-altern-research)

## Foundational Affiliation

Project IMACE is a *cross-ARC (**A**ltern **R**esearch **C**ollective) flagship research program* within THE ALTERN Research. It is primarily anchored in:

- **The ArcBMM** _(The Altern Research Collective of Brains, Minds & Machines)_   
  - Contact: [arcbmm@thealtern.xyz](mailto:arcbmm@thealtern.xyz)

ArcBMM serves as the principal research collective providing foundational support in the domains of cognitive science, neuroscience, and machine intelligence.

## Collaborative and Supporting Collectives

Project IMACE operates as an integrative research system and is supported through collaboration with the following collectives:

- **ArcAMI** — The ARC of Advanced Machine Intelligence

- **ArcPsyCog** — The ARC of Psychology and Cognitive Science

- **ArcCCS** — The ARC of Computational and Computer Science

- **ArcDevSys** — The ARC of DevOps and Systems Research  
  - Contact: [arcdevsys@thealtern.xyz](mailto:arcdevsys@thealtern.xyz)

These collectives contribute domain-specific expertise, infrastructure, and research alignment within their respective areas.

## Role of Affiliation

Affiliation within THE ALTERN Research ecosystem provides:

- interdisciplinary collaboration  
- research support and alignment  
- access to collective expertise and infrastructure  

Affiliation does not imply governance authority over the [Structure](STRUCTURE.md)

## Independence

IMACE operates as an independent research initiative.

While it is structurally connected to THE ALTERN Research ecosystem, all decisions regarding:

- research direction  
- project structure  
- official releases  
- policy and governance  

remain under the authority of the IMACE coordination structure.

## Functional Position

Project IMACE functions as an integrative research system aimed at constructing cognitively grounded artificial intelligence architectures through coordinated, cross-disciplinary collaboration.
