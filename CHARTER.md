# Project IMACE Charter

## 1. Overview

Project IMACE (Integrated Modular Architecture for Cognitive Emulation) is an independent, open research initiative for cognitively grounded intelligent systems.

---

## 2. Purpose

Project IMACE advances research on cognitive emulation (CoEm) through open and structured inquiry.

See [Research Scope](RESEARCH_SCOPE.md).

---

## 3. Identity

Project IMACE is an open, independent research community with coordinated governance.

---

## 4. Foundational Position

Project IMACE adopts a research orientation centered on cognitive emulation, interpretability, modularity, and empirical validation.

See [Research Scope](RESEARCH_SCOPE.md).

---

## 5. Governance and Authority

Project IMACE is governed through a coordination structure responsible for:

- direction  
- contribution approval  
- official releases  
- structural consistency  

Only approved outputs are considered **official Project IMACE releases**.

See [Structure](STRUCTURE.md).

---

## 6. Contributions

All contributions must align with:

- [Research Scope](RESEARCH_SCOPE.md)  
- [Collaboration](COLLABORATION.md)  
- [Intellectual Property Policy](IP_POLICY.md)  

---

## 7. Affiliation and Collaboration

Project IMACE maintains structured collaboration while preserving independence.

See:
- [Affiliation](AFFILIATION.md)  
- [Collaboration](COLLABORATION.md)

---

## 8. Intellectual Property

Use and redistribution are governed by:

- [Intellectual Property Policy](IP_POLICY.md)  
- repository-specific licenses  

---

## 9. Licensing

Licensing follows a repository-level model.

See [Licensing Policy](LICENSING.md).

---

## 10. Sponsorship

Support does not imply control.

See [Sponsorship](SPONSORSHIP.md).

---

## 11. Amendments

This Charter may be updated through the Project IMACE coordination structure.

---

## 12. Closing

Project IMACE operates as an open, structured research initiative advancing modular, interpretable, and empirically grounded cognitive systems.
